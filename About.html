Rock Ridge TSA

Rock Ridge TSA is an after-school program hosted by Rock Ridge and partnered with the Technology Student Association (TSA). TSA is a world of innovative thinkers who collaborate and problem solve in real life applications. Rock Ridge TSA focuses on combining these thinkers in the STEM (Science, Technology, and Engineering, and Mathematics) world to engage in over 50 competitions at the regional, state, and national competition.

Rock Ridge High School CTE

Rock Ridge High School (RRHS) has a various advanced careers & technical education (CTE) programs. Their CTE programs help prepare today's students for their future education and workplace experiences by offering lots of hands-on experience, and lots of practice in a professional workplace environment.

CTE Program Areas

The Career & Technical Education department at Rock Ridge High School consists of four program areas:

  • Business & Information Technology

  • Family & Consumer Sciences

  • Marketing Education

  • Technology & Engineering Education
