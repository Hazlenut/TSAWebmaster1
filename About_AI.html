About Artificial Intelligence


What is Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence is the theory and development of computer systems able to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence and cognitive functions, such as learning, decision making, problem-solving, speech recognition, translation between languages, and visual acuity.

A more simple definition for artificial intelligence is that it is the ability of a computer program or a machine to think and learn.


What is a Robot?

A robot is commonly defined as a machine capable of carrying out a complex series of actions automatically, especially one programmable by a computer.

What are the potential future uses of a robot with artificial intelligence?

The potential future uses of a robot with artificial intelligence are personal, commercial, and recreational.


Personal

Utilibot can help answer questions with a built in search engine. It can also
clean the floor and mow the lawn. Lastly, a surveillance system was
made which includes motion detection, machine vision, etc.



Commercial

Robots have many functions that can be used in a work setting. Since the robot is programmed with an internal GPS, it can serve as a taxi driver of help with any map related issues. Furthermore, robots can take the place of humans in dangerous jobs such as construction work. The benefit of having a robot is the one time cost and a few maintenance cost rather than a yearly salary which could potentially benefit a company.



Recreational

Utilibot has many features that provides entertainment to users. The robot is capable of playing games such as chess or checkers. Furthermore, the robot can share stories as well as make videos.



Research

In research laboratories, robots can be used to conduct experiments that can be fatal to human health. Additionally, robots can calculate data faster than humans and store more data inside their system.




What are the future safety concerns of Artificial Intelligence?

Robots, as advanced as modern technology brings us, are still machines and can malfunction which results in dangerous results. People need to be wary about the possibility of the robots system having a reaction from the circuits or any other malfunction.
